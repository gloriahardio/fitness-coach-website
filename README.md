## How to Customize

### Edit Your Content
1. Click on `index.html` in your GitHub repository  
2. Click the pencil icon to edit  
3. Update the text in the sections with your information  
4. Commit changes

### Change Colors/Styling
1. Open `styles.css`  
2. Look for the header background color: `#667eea` and `#764ba2`  
3. Change these hex colors to your preferred colors  
4. The site will automatically update!

### Add Your Contact Information
Update the email in the Contact section with your email address

## Enable GitHub Pages (Make it Live!)

1. Go to your repository Settings  
2. Click **Pages** in the left sidebar  
3. Under Source, select **Deploy from a branch**  
4. Choose **main** branch  
5. Click **Save**

Your site will be live at: https://gloriahardio.github.io/fitness-coach-website/
